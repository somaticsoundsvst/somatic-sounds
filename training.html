Step into the world of sound & vibration

This three-day hybrid, online / in-person one on one training is designed to give you the knowledge, confidence, and hands-on experience to begin your journey as a sound healing practitioner.

Whether you're completely new to sound healing or looking to deepen your practice, this certification course will guide you into the art and science of working with Himalayan singing bowls — with clarity, confidence, and purpose.

You'll leave with a certification, practical skills, ongoing support, and — optionally — a personally selected set of high-quality Himalayan singing bowls for your practice.

Curriculum

What you'll explore

A comprehensive, layered journey — from the science to the sacred.

1

What is Sound Healing & Its Benefits

Understanding how sound impacts the mind, body, and spirit — the foundations of why this works.

2

History of Singing Bowls

Tracing the roots of these sacred instruments and their traditional uses across cultures and centuries.

3

Singing Bowls — Types, Mallets & Care

Learn to identify different types of bowls, choose the right mallets, and care for your instruments.

4

The Science of Sound

An introduction to vibration, frequency, resonance, and why sound works as a healing modality.

5

Chakras & Mantras

Exploring the energetic body and how sound connects with the chakra system and Vedic traditions.

6

Playing Singing Bowls

Hands-on training in foundational playing techniques — rim play, striking, and tonal layering.

7

Vibrational Sound Therapy (VST)

Hands-on training for one-on-one sessions. Learn to place bowls around and directly on the body, drawing from the Somatic Resonance System alongside Himalayan and Vedic techniques.

8

Introduction to Gongs

Discover different types of gongs and learn basic gong-playing techniques.

9

Facilitating a Sound Bath

Guidance on how to structure, open, and close sound sessions for groups of any size.

10

Sonic Mastery & Ongoing Support

Deepening your relationship with sound as a tool for transformation — and continued guidance after the course.

Our Approach

A focused lineage

This training is dedicated entirely to Himalayan singing bowls. We don't cover crystal bowls, handpans, or other modalities — mastery, in our view, comes from going deep with one lineage of instruments rather than sampling across many.

Is This For You?

You're in the right place if...

You're called to share sound

You've experienced the power of sound healing and feel a pull to bring it to others — but don't know where to start.

You're a wellness professional

Yoga teachers, therapists, coaches, and bodyworkers looking to add a profound somatic tool to their existing practice.

You're on a personal journey

You don't need to become a practitioner — some people take this training purely to deepen their own healing and self-practice.

Training participants have the option to purchase a personally curated set of high-quality Himalayan singing bowls — ideal for starting your personal or professional practice.

Each set is selected by Vivek himself, with attention to tone, resonance, and energetic quality. Sourced directly from Nepal.
