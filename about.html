About

Vivek Chugh

sound healer · lightworker · instructor

Vivek's journey into sound healing began in 2012, when he experienced his first crystal bowl sound bath at his mother's energy healing group. The resonance of those bowls left a lasting imprint, planting a seed for future exploration.

In 2020, following a struggle with long COVID, Vivek delved deeper into alternative and holistic therapies. This quest for wellbeing led him to Nepal and India, where he studied Vipassana meditation, Dynamic meditation, and Mahayana Buddhism. In Nepal, he also experienced firsthand the profound effects of Himalayan singing bowl meditations. Guided by various masters, Vivek immersed himself in the study of sound healing methods. The practice has brought him a deep sense of well-being, and he's passionate about sharing its benefits with others.

Today, through Somatic Sounds, he offers private Vibrational Sound Therapy sessions, group sound baths, workshops, retreats, and sound healing training, helping others find their own path to healing through sound.

The Tools

More than 40 handcrafted bowls

Each bowl was personally selected for its quality, resonance, and tonal character. Many were sourced directly from Nepal — hand-hammered by craftsmen using methods passed down through generations.

Together, they create a rich spectrum of frequencies and overtones — a somatic soundscape that offers far more than a typical sound bath.
